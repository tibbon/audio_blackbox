Reliability and polish across the recording engine.

• The menu bar icon now pulses while recording so you can see at a glance that capture is live.
• Onboarding window no longer clips on the recording-mode step and reopens to the foreground if you've dismissed it.
• Faster app launch — the menu bar appears immediately instead of waiting on a folder probe.
• Smoother level meters during long sessions.
• More precise error reporting — failures from the audio engine now surface specific reasons (device unavailable, low disk space, etc.) instead of a generic message.
• Hardened audio-sample conversion against malformed input from unusual devices — bad samples can no longer freeze the level meter or skew silence detection.
• Smaller download (~26% reduction).
• Updated to the latest Rust toolchain and audio dependencies.
