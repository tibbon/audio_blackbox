Localization, accessibility, and reliability improvements.

• The app is now localizable, with number, file-size, and bitrate values formatted for your region.
• Recordings no longer clip the moment audio begins — the sound that opens the silence gate is captured from the start.
• Improved fidelity when saving 16-bit files (dither on conversion).
• Accessibility: VoiceOver support across onboarding, the level meter, the channel grid, and the menu; Reduce Motion and Dynamic Type are honored.
• More reliable recovery from disk-write failures and across sleep/wake; recordings now save to the app's own folder by default.
