Reliability and accessibility polish.

• Settings now opens via the standard ⌘, shortcut from anywhere in the app and inherits macOS's normal Settings window behavior.
• First-run onboarding correctly remembers your choice of recording folder — no more "Output Directory Unavailable" prompt on second launch when you accepted the default.
• "Recording Started" notifications appear reliably on the first auto-record-on-launch, instead of being dropped before notification permission was requested.
• Onboarding accessibility: VoiceOver users can now navigate the recording-mode picker as a single-select group, and step back through completed onboarding steps via the indicator dots.
• Level-meter VoiceOver readouts are throttled to threshold crossings (Silent / Low / Moderate / Hot / Clipping) instead of streaming a new dB reading every screen tick.
• Internal cleanups: removed leftover scaffolding code, modernized the build pipeline, hardened the supply chain.
